
  Even though a spirited debate is good, I like debates to be a cordial, clear argumentation of the resolution at hand.

I zero in on who does the best job overall rather than technicalities or points missed by the opponent. I look for the affirmative speaker to offer a strong position supporting the resolution, and the negative speaker to either present an opposing position or offer a refutation of the affirmative position. Concentration on the main question of the resolution is very important to me.

Both, style of presentation, combined with clarity of speech are also important to me. Avoiding unnecessary “spread” and communicating clearly goes a long way, also treating your opponent with respect is paramount.
