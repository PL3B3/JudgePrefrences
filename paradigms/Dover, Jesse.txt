
  I don't really have a "philosophy" for judging, per se, but I do have (dis)likes:

Likes:
Concision and Clarity are Paramount.
LD is not PF/CX, otherwise it would be called PF/CX. So keep it about values and philosophy, not cards. "Traditional"
Speed within your ability is fine, but don't do it if you can't handle it. In the end, style is more powerful than shoving the most information into the allotted time. Use the most vital pieces well!

Dislikes:
Rudeness, not tolerated
Overly complicated critiques
Saying I should turn a point or draw something over the flow rather than just turning it and focusing on quality and style.

Remember to have fun. Once you lose that, what's the point.
